Good Tea. After water, tea is the most widely consumed beverage in the world. It is made by adding boiling water to a pot with tea leaves. It originated in East Asia where it was used for medicinal purposes. Over time it has spread throughout the globe and now serves many purposes whether it be for its various tastes, helping with relaxation and sleep or even boosting your energy. Tea is a wonderful thing that everyone should enjoy, so explore different types of teas here and find out what they can do for you.
